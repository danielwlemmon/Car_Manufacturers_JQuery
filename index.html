Tesla is the leading electric car manufacturer in the USA. They currently offer 4 models, that account for nearly 70% of the electric vehicle market. They are rapidly growing, posting over 70% year of year growth the past 4 quarters.

Ford's entry into the electric vehicle market includes the stunning Mach-E. In 2021 they revealed plans to make the Ford F-150 lightning. Both of these offerings are sure to satisfy people converting from internal combustion engine vehicles.

Rivian's electric truck the R1T is pack full of features worthy of the self-branded description of 'Adventure Vehicle'. Rivian has tens of thousands of orders, and although they are havingn difficulty scaling their production, they certainly created a desirable vehicle.

Polestar is a swedish automotive brand owned by the Chinese company Geely. Their Polestar 2 is their flagship vehicle in the USA. With its sharp lines, and unique design, it's sure to draw a lot of attention. Boasting 400hp, and a range of up to 233 miles, it has certainly claimed its place as a real EV competitor.
